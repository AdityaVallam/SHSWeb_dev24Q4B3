High School JJ

Justin Jefferson went to Destrehan High School and was one of their top recievers there.

He came out of High School catching 45 passes for 8 TDS in his senior year.

Although he showed a lot of talent, he was rated to be only a 3 star recruit and many colleges skipped on him.

The only D1 offer that Jefferson got was from Louisiana State University(LSU) and he committed to go there at the end of his senior year in High School.
